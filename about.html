Our Story

Started in 2012 as a small refrigeration repair service, we have grown into a trusted mobile appliance repair team serving households and businesses with professional, on-site solutions. Our technicians specialise in repairing fridges, washing machines, air conditioning, and electrical appliances.

Our reputation is built on reliability, quality workmanship, and exceptional customer service. We take pride in providing convenient mobile repairs that keep homes and businesses running smoothly.

Our Mission

To deliver fast, professional, and trustworthy mobile repair services you can depend on. We focus on honest pricing, clear communication, and getting your appliances back to working condition with minimal downtime.

Our Values
