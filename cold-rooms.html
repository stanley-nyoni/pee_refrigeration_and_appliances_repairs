Common Cold Room Problems We Fix

  • Temperature fluctuations: Room not maintaining consistent temperature
  • Compressor failure: Main cooling system breakdown
  • Evaporator coil issues: Frost buildup or coil freezing
  • Condenser problems: Poor heat rejection
  • Refrigerant leaks: Loss of cooling capacity
  • Control system faults: Thermostat or controller malfunctions
  • Door seal failures: Cold air leakage
  • Fan motor failures: Evaporator or condenser fan issues
  • Insulation problems: Moisture or damage to insulation
  • Electrical faults: Wiring, relay, or contactor problems
