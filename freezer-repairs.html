Professional Freezer Repairs

Is your freezer not freezing properly? Building up too much ice? Making strange noises? Our certified technicians specialize in repairing all types of freezers - from household chest freezers to commercial upright freezers.

Common Freezer Problems We Fix

  • Not freezing: Freezer not maintaining proper freezing temperature
  • Excessive frost: Ice buildup preventing proper operation
  • Defrost system failure: Freezer not defrosting automatically
  • Compressor issues: Freezer running constantly or not at all
  • Temperature control: Inconsistent freezing temperatures
  • Door seal problems: Worn or damaged door gaskets causing cold air loss
  • Strange noises: Clicking, buzzing, or humming sounds
  • Water leakage: Water pooling inside or under the freezer
  • Fan motor failure: Evaporator or condenser fan not working
  • Thermostat issues: Temperature regulation problems

Freezer Maintenance Tip

Regular defrosting (when ice buildup exceeds 1/4 inch) improves efficiency and extends your freezer's life. Clean condenser coils every 6 months for optimal performance.
