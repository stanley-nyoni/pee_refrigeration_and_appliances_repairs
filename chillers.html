Commercial & Industrial Chiller Solutions

We specialize in chiller systems for commercial and industrial applications across Gauteng. From process cooling to air conditioning applications, we provide comprehensive chiller services for businesses.

Types of Chillers We Service

Water-Cooled Chillers

Using cooling towers for heat rejection, ideal for large capacity applications

Air-Cooled Chillers

Using ambient air for heat rejection, easier installation and maintenance

Screw Chillers

Large capacity chillers using screw compressors for industrial applications

Scroll Chillers

Using scroll compressors for medium capacity applications

Common Chiller Problems We Fix

  • Compressor failure: Mechanical or electrical compressor issues
  • Refrigerant leaks: Loss of refrigerant affecting cooling capacity
  • Condenser problems: Scaling, fouling, or airflow issues
  • Evaporator issues: Freezing, scaling, or tube leaks
  • Control system faults: PLC, controller, or sensor problems
  • Electrical faults: Motor, starter, or wiring issues
  • Water flow problems: Pump failures or flow restrictions
  • Freeze protection: Glycol systems and freeze protection
  • Capacity control: Unloading or capacity regulation issues
  • Safety controls: High pressure, low pressure, or temperature controls
