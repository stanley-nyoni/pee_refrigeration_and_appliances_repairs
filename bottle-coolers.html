Common Bottle Cooler Problems

  • Temperature issues: Not cooling to proper beverage temperature
  • Condensation: Excessive condensation on glass or interior
  • Display lighting: LED or fluorescent light failures
  • Door seal problems: Cold air leakage or door not sealing
  • Fan noise: Loud or noisy fan operation
  • Compressor failure: Unit not cooling or constantly running
  • Defrost system: Ice buildup affecting performance
  • Control panel: Temperature display or control issues
  • Water leakage: Water pooling inside or under unit
  • Shelving problems: Broken or damaged bottle shelves

Energy Saving Tip

Keep bottle cooler doors closed as much as possible. Each time the door opens, 30-50% of cold air escapes. Consider installing strip curtains on frequently opened units.
