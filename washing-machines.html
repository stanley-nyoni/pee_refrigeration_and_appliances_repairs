Professional Washing Machine Repairs

Is your washing machine not spinning? Leaking water? Making strange noises? Our certified technicians specialize in repairing all types of washing machines - from front-load and top-load washers to commercial laundry equipment.

Common Washing Machine Problems We Fix

  • Not spinning: Drum not spinning or spinning slowly
  • Not draining: Water not draining from machine
  • Water leakage: Water pooling under or around machine
  • Not filling: Water not entering the machine
  • Strange noises: Grinding, banging, or squealing sounds
  • Door lock issues: Door not locking or unlocking
  • Control panel problems: Buttons not working or error codes
  • Vibration: Excessive shaking during spin cycle
  • Detergent issues: Detergent not dispensing properly
  • Motor failure: Machine not starting or stopping mid-cycle

Preventive Maintenance Tips

Monthly Maintenance
  • Clean detergent drawer
  • Wipe door seal clean
  • Check drain filter
  • Run hot wash cycle with vinegar
Every 6 Months
  • Clean drain pump filter
  • Check hoses for cracks
  • Inspect power cord
  • Level the machine

Quick Troubleshooting

If your washing machine won't start: 1) Check if it's plugged in, 2) Ensure water supply is on, 3) Check circuit breaker, 4) Make sure door is properly closed. If problems persist, call us for professional diagnosis.
