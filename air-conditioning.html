Common AC Problems We Fix

  • Not cooling: AC blowing warm air
  • Poor airflow: Weak air circulation
  • Water leakage: Water dripping from unit
  • Strange noises: Rattling, buzzing, or grinding sounds
  • Frozen coils: Ice buildup on evaporator coils
  • Compressor failure: Unit not starting or tripping breaker
  • Thermostat issues: Temperature control problems
  • Fan problems: Indoor or outdoor fan failures
  • Refrigerant leaks: Loss of cooling capacity
  • Electrical faults: Wiring or control board issues

Energy Saving Tips

Set your thermostat to 24°C in summer for optimal comfort and energy efficiency. Every degree lower increases energy consumption by 6-8%. Use ceiling fans with your AC to feel 4°C cooler while using less energy.
