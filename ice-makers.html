Common Ice Maker Problems

  • Not making ice: Unit not producing any ice
  • Slow ice production: Reduced ice output
  • Small or misshapen ice: Ice cubes too small or malformed
  • Cloudy ice: Ice appears cloudy or white
  • Water leakage: Water pooling around unit
  • Strange noises: Unusual sounds during operation
  • Dispenser issues: Ice not dispensing properly
  • Water valve problems: Water not entering machine
  • Thermostat failure: Temperature regulation issues
  • Mineral buildup: Scale affecting performance

Water Quality Importance

Poor water quality is the #1 cause of ice maker problems. We recommend installing water filters and scheduling regular descaling to prevent mineral buildup and ensure clean, clear ice.
