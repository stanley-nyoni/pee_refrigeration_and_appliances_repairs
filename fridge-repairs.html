Professional Refrigerator Repairs

Is your refrigerator not cooling properly? Making strange noises? Leaking water? Our certified technicians can diagnose and fix any refrigerator issue quickly and efficiently.

Common Fridge Problems We Fix

  • Not cooling: Fridge or freezer section not maintaining temperature
  • Excessive frost: Ice buildup in freezer or fridge section
  • Water leakage: Water pooling inside or under the fridge
  • Strange noises: Clicking, buzzing, humming, or rattling sounds
  • Ice maker issues: Not making ice or dispensing properly
  • Door seal problems: Worn or damaged door gaskets
  • Light not working: Interior light not turning on
  • Temperature control: Inconsistent cooling or freezing
  • Compressor issues: Fridge running constantly or not at all
  • Condenser problems: Poor heat dissipation

Quick Tip

If your fridge stops cooling, try these steps first: 1) Check if it's plugged in, 2) Ensure temperature settings are correct, 3) Clean the condenser coils at the back. If problems persist, call us immediately to prevent food spoilage.
