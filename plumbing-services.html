Water Saving Tips & Leak Detection

Check for Hidden Leaks

To check for hidden leaks: 1) Turn off all water-using appliances, 2) Read your water meter, 3) Wait 2 hours without using any water, 4) Read meter again. If it has moved, you have a leak. A dripping tap can waste 20,000+ liters per year!
