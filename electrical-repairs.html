Professional Electrical Repair Services

Experiencing electrical problems? Tripping breakers? Faulty outlets? Appliance electrical issues? Our certified electricians specialize in diagnosing and repairing all types of electrical faults for homes and businesses across Gauteng.

Electrical Safety Warning

Electrical work can be dangerous and should only be performed by qualified professionals. Never attempt DIY electrical repairs. If you experience sparking, burning smells, or frequent tripping, turn off power at the mains and call us immediately.

Common Electrical Problems We Fix

  • Tripping circuit breakers: Frequent or unexplained tripping
  • Faulty outlets/sockets: Not working, loose, or sparking
  • Lighting problems: Flickering lights or complete failure
  • Power surges: Appliances damaged by voltage spikes
  • Wiring faults: Old, damaged, or faulty wiring
  • Earth leakage trips: Earth fault circuit interrupter problems
  • Appliance electrical faults: Internal wiring issues in appliances
  • Switch problems: Light switches not working properly
  • DB board issues: Distribution board/fuse box problems
  • Voltage fluctuations: Inconsistent power supply

Electrical Safety Tips

1) Test your earth leakage monthly by pressing the test button. 2) Don't overload outlets - use power strips with surge protection. 3) Keep electrical cords away from heat and water. 4) Unplug appliances before cleaning. 5) Have your electrical system inspected every 10 years.
