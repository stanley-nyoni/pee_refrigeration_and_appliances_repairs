Common Under-Counter Unit Problems

  • Not cooling: Unit not maintaining proper temperature
  • Excessive noise: Loud operation from compressor or fans
  • Water leakage: Water pooling inside or under the unit
  • Door alignment: Doors not closing properly or misaligned
  • Light issues: Interior light not working
  • Temperature fluctuations: Inconsistent cooling
  • Compressor failure: Unit not running or constantly running
  • Fan problems: Evaporator or condenser fan issues
  • Defrost system: Excessive frost buildup
  • Control panel: Temperature control or display problems

Installation Tip

Proper ventilation is crucial for under-counter units. Ensure at least 2-3cm clearance at the back and sides for airflow. Never install in an enclosed space without ventilation.
